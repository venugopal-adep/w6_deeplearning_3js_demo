Perceptron Concept

Single Neuron: The perceptron is the simplest form of a neural network, consisting of just one neuron.

Linear Classification: It separates classes using a linear decision boundary (hyperplane in 3D).

Weighted Sum: Combines inputs using weights: net = w₁x₁ + w₂x₂ + w₃x₃ + b

Activation: Applies a function to the weighted sum to produce the final output.

Learning: Adjusts weights based on training examples using gradient descent.
